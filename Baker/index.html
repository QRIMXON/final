Concept

Dawg Snax are a highly conscious treat any way you cut it. The ingredients are simple organic whole foods, and the main mission of Dawg Snax is to

GET SHELTER DOGS ADOPTED!

The Process

Dawg Snax are hand made locally with high-quality vegan ingredients. Care is put into making our treats easy on your pups tummy and allergen-friendly.

The Truth

We are about our values! Vegan owned and operated, Dawg Snax was founded in efforts to get the precious pups from shelters we frequent to a good home.
