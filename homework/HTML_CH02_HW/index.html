St. Louis Skateboarding

Skateboarding has arguably been around for the past 75 years, with the first device that resembled a skateboard showing up in the WWII era. It gained some popularity in the late 60's, and once again in the late 90's thanks to technology, letting millions of kids experience what skateboarding was all about without having to leave their living rooms. Thank you Tony Hawk!
