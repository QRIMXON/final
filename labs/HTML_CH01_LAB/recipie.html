Mac & Cheese

    Ingredients

  • Elbow Noodles- 0.5 lbs
  • Milk- 1 cup
  • Shredded Cheese- 0.25 lbs
  • Buter- 2 Table Spoons
    Cooking Instructions
  1. Bring noodles to a boil.
  2. Strain noodles, then add butter and milk.
  3. Lastly, sprinkle in shredded cheese while stirring until full .25 lbs is completely melted.
